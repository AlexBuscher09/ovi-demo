About us

Going into more detail,


  • Our main activities are, as follows:

    The Transhipment of Oil Products

    Provision of Wholesale Petroleum Products to the Domestic and EU Markets

    Manufacture of Luboils and Additives

    Provision of Services Related to Oil Products

  • OVI is situated in the industrial area of Riga, on Tvaika str., 35/37a, and consists of three technologically linked sites, with excellent transport links.
  • The company has all the necessary permits and licences to operate in the following main areas: transhipment of oil products, wholesale in the domestic market and in EU countries, the production of lubricating oils and additives and the provision of related services.
  • The company is energetically modernizing and expanding its transhipment capacity. A project to increase the capacity of transhipment up to 1 million tons per year is developed.
